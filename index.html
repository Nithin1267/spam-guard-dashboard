About SpamSense

How the detection works under the hood.
1

Keyword Scoring

Each email is scanned for known spam phrases. Every match adds a weighted score based on how strong a signal it is.

2

Heuristic Rules

SpamSense also checks for excessive ALL-CAPS, multiple exclamation marks, and suspicious links to boost the score.

3

Verdict & Risk

The combined score is normalized to a 0–100% confidence, then mapped to a verdict (Spam/Safe) and risk level (Low/Medium/High).
